Projects

Serrated Grain Generator (MATLAB → DXF)

Wrote simpsons.m, a Voronoi-based generator that pairs parallel edges and applies band-limited serrations; exports clean DXF for FEA/CAD. Tunable amplitude and edge selection enable realistic serration statistics for downstream meshing.

Stress Localization Study in Serrated Boundaries

Designed COMSOL workflows to compare serrated vs. smooth grain boundaries. Implemented edge datasets + cut-line evaluations to quantify peak/average stress through serrations; explored material contrast between grains and intergranular films.
